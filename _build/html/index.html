Getting help¶

Having trouble? We’d like to help!

  • Try the FAQ – it’s got answers to many common questions.
  • Looking for specific information? Try the Index, Module Index or the Reference Manual.
  • Search for information in the XL Users, or look in the Tips and Tricks.
  • Report bugs with Deployit in our support site.
  • For urgent problems, the XebiaLabs support team can also be reached by phone
